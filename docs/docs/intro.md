## What is ZendFi?

ZendFi is a **crypto payment API** built on the Solana blockchain that enables merchants to:

- Accept payments in **USDC**, **SOL**, and **USDT**
- Create **recurring subscriptions** with automated billing
- Set up **escrow payments** for secure transactions
- Offer **installment plans** for high-ticket items
- Generate **professional invoices** with email delivery
- Create **reusable payment links** for social media and e-commerce
- Configure **payment splits** for revenue sharing
- Manage wallets with **MPC passkey** technology

## Features Overview

### Payments
Accept one-time crypto payments with QR codes and payment links. Support for USDC, SOL, and USDT with automatic price conversion.

### Subscriptions
Create recurring billing plans with flexible intervals (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly). Free trials, automatic billing, and webhook notifications included.

### Escrow
Secure high-value transactions with third-party escrow. Funds held safely until conditions are met - perfect for marketplaces and freelance work.

### Installments
Break large payments into manageable installments. Late fee support, grace periods, and automatic payment tracking.

### Invoices
Professional invoicing with line items, due dates, and email delivery. Automatic invoice numbering and payment tracking.

### Payment Links
Reusable payment URLs for social media, e-commerce, and email campaigns. Beautiful hosted checkout pages included.

### Payment Splits
Configure revenue sharing between multiple wallets. Perfect for marketplaces, affiliates, and partner programs.

### Webhooks
Real-time notifications for all payment events. 23 event types across 8 categories with signature verification.
